🥨Texas German Food🍺

Below you will find a list of places to eat German food in Texas.

Places are located in New Braunfels and Fredericksburg, Texas.


Fredericksburg Restaurants

Otto's

Ottos

Otto’s German Bistro is a nice cozy restaurant featuring German style cuisine. The food made by this restaurant is locally farmed and organic. The culinary team creates a dynamic menu which includes seasonal produce giving each dish a unique flavor and experience. The restaurant also includes a variety of artisan cocktails, craft beers, and local wines!

What's Otto's hours of operation and location?

Nightly 4:00 p.m. to 10:30 p.m

316 East Austin Street, Fredericksburg, TX, 78624

Personal favorite dish is the Duck schnitzel.


New Braunfels, Texas Restaurants

Krause's Cafe

Krause's Cafe

Krause’s café is the perfect place to grab a local brew and enjoy the German classics. This restaurant/biergartens provides an open and uplifting atmosphere with a variety of dishes including schnitzel and bratwurst!

What's Krause's Cafe hours of operation and location?

Monday-Thursday 7am – 10pm (Breakfast 7-11am), Friday, Saturday 7am – 11pm (Breakfast 7-11am), Sunday 8am – 9pm,(Breakfast 8am – 11am with a limited breakfast menu until 2pm)

148 S Castell Ave, New Braunfels, TX, 78130

Personal favorite dish is the Reuben.
